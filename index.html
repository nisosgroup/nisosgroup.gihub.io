Nisos Group is a premier risk advisory and research organization specializing in cyber and operational risk. Nisos operators have honed their abilities in the most demanding environments of the last decade and they deliver an unmatched concentration of expertise in risk mitigation. Comprised of former Intelligence Community and Special Forces officers skilled in overt, covert, and clandestine operations, our staff is highly trained, discreet, and uncompromising.

Our Remote Operators apply their ingenuity to assess risks to digital infrastructure, source code, third-party affiliations, and supply chains. When existing tools fall short, they code custom solutions to solve unique problems. They have significant expertise in identifying risks through non-public information on potential partners, and merger/acquisition targets; and experience obfuscating client interests and strategies to protect their competitive advantage.

Our Special Operators bring unmatched experience working discreetly in austere and non-permissive environments. Experts at thwarting insider threats and unconventional challenges from state and non-state actors, Nisos’ Special Operators provide peace of mind and return on investment.

Our Technical Analysts are drawn from both financial institutions and the Intelligence Community. Taking inputs from Remote and Special Operators, our Analysts prioritize risks and distill action plans to determine impact to clients' enterprises. Our team possesses the rare ability to translate technical details into a comprehensive understanding of the business impact and bottom line.
